About

who / what / why

A decade of shipping for teams big and small: OpenPPG, Throwflame, RVshare, and a long tail of independents.

I was a founding engineer at RVshare, where the team went on to raise over $100M from firms like KKR. Since then I have split my time between small product shops and my own builds, writing the code, shaping the business, and staying close to hardware and aviation.

My edge is working across the whole stack a company has: the storefront, the firmware, the ops inbox, and the spreadsheet that keeps it all together. That range matters when the product spans checkout, telemetry, fulfillment, and field testing.

Earlier

My first job was at Movable, building wrist-worn activity monitors with a sync client and cloud tools for reviewing movement and competition. It was wearable hardware before the Apple Watch existed.
