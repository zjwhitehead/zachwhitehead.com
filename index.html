About

who / what / why

A decade of shipping for teams big and small: OpenPPG, Throwflame, RVshare, and a long tail of independents.

I was founding engineer at RVshare, where the team went on to raise over $100M from firms like KKR. Since then I have split my time between small product shops and my own builds, writing the code, shaping the business, and on good weekends, flying.

My edge is working across the whole stack a company has: the storefront, the firmware, the ops inbox, and the spreadsheet that keeps it all together. A checkout that converts, a controller that does not brick in the field, a marketplace that keeps both sides happy. Quiet wins.
